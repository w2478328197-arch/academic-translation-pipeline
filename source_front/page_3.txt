Check Out HKPropel!
You will notice references throughout this version of Essentials of
Strength Training and Conditioning, Fifth Edition, to HKPropel online
content. HKPropel includes a collection of video clips of 25 resistance
training exercises for use in understanding and performing correct
exercise techniques. Plus, lab activities are provided to give students
hands-on practice with testing and evaluation. The 20 forms make
completing and submitting lab assignments easy. An access code for
HKPropel may have been included with your purchase of this ebook.
To access your online materials through HKPropel using the code
you received when you purchased your ebook, follow the steps
below.
NOTE: If you are an instructor, use the HKPropel access code
provided by your Human Kinetics sales rep. instead of the code you
received with your ebook purchase, and disregard the remaining
steps listed here.
If it’s your first time using HKPropel:
1. Visit HKPropel.HumanKinetics.com.
2. Click the “New user? Register here” link on the opening
screen to register for an account and redeem your one-time-
use access code.
3. Follow the onscreen prompts to create your HKPropel
account. Use a valid email address as your username to
ensure you receive important system updates and to help us
find your account if you ever need assistance.
4. Enter the access code exactly as it was provided to you,
