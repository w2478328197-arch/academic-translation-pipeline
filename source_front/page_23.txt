Updates to the Fifth Edition
This fifth edition remains the single best resource for the key
theories, concepts, and scientific principles of strength training and
conditioning. Its scope, precision, and dependability make it a
definitive reference for strength and conditioning professionals to
consult for information on the structure and function of body
systems, training adaptations, testing and evaluation, exercise
technique, program design, and the organization and administration
of 
facilities.
