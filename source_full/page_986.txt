  13   SEATED CALF (HEEL) RAISE
(MACHINE)
Starting Position
Sit erect on the seat and place the balls of the feet on the
nearest edge of the step with the legs and feet hip-width apart
and parallel to each other.
Lower the thigh–knee pad so it firmly presses against the knees
and front of the lower thigh area.
Plantar flex the ankles to remove the supports.
Allow the heels to lower to a comfortable, stretched position.
All repetitions begin from this position.
