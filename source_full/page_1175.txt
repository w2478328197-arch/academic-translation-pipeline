Allow momentum to raise the kettlebell to eye level, keeping the
elbows extended and the back in a neutral spine position.
Downward and Backward Movement Phase
Allow the kettlebell to drop into the downswing; flex the hips
and knees to absorb the weight. Keep the elbows fully extended
and the back neutral.
Continue the downward-then-backward movement until the
kettlebell passes under then behind the body, then begin the
upward movement phase for the next repetition.
