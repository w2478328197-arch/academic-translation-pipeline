Strongman Exercises
  9   TIRE FLIP
Starting Position
Begin facing the tire with the feet hip- to shoulder-width apart.
Squat down and lean into the tire so that the chin and the
anterior deltoids are resting on the tire and the feet are
positioned back far enough to maintain a neutral spine position.
Keeping the arms outside the knees and the elbows fully
extended, grasp the tire with a supinated grip.
All repetitions begin from this position.
Upward Movement Phase
Begin by extending the knees and hips and plantar flexing the
ankles while pushing the tire forward.
Move forward explosively toward the tire by taking two or three
steps.
Continue this movement until the body is aligned in a 45-degree
angle to the tire.
Consider flexing the hip and knee of one leg fully to drive the
knee upward and toward the tire.
Immediately after striking the tire with the thigh (if done),
reorient the hands into a pronated grip.
