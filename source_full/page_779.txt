Maximum Muscular Power (High-Speed Strength)
  4   ISOMETRIC MID-THIGH PULL
The isometric mid-thigh pull (IMTP) is a multijoint maximal isometric strength test.
Equipment
Force plate (or two adjacent force plates) that samples at a minimum of 1,000 Hz, but it can
also be conducted using a strain gauge or crane scale that samples at 100 to 133 Hz (29, 32)
An immovable fixed bar to measure peak force and time-dependent variables such as the rate
of force development; this specific testing rack is designed for isometric testing and allows for
stepwise adjustments to the bar height. If a strain gauge is used, a handle is attached to the
strain gauge with a chain.
Weightlifting straps
Computer or tablet with specialized software that allows for the collection and analysis of
force–time curve data
Goniometer to record the knee and hip angles
Personnel
One tester/recorder
