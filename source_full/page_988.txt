   14   FLAT BARBELL BENCH PRESS
(AND DUMBBELL VARIATION)
This exercise can also be performed with two dumbbells, using a
closed, pronated grip. When using dumbbells, the spotter assists by
spotting the athlete’s forearms near the wrists instead of the bar.
Starting Position: Athlete
Lie in a supine position on a bench in the five-point body
contact position.
Place the body on the bench so that the eyes are below the
racked bar.
Grasp the bar with a closed, pronated grip slightly wider than
shoulder-width apart.
