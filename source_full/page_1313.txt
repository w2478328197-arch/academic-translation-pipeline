Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance on one foot.
The non-hopping leg is held in a stationary position with the knee
flexed during the exercise.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Explosively hop up. Pull the knee of the hopping
leg to the chest, grasp the knee with both hands, and release before
landing.
Downward movement: Dorsiflex the ankles in preparation for an
active flat-foot landing. Land in the starting position, and
immediately repeat the jump using the same leg. Complete a set,
then repeat with the opposite leg after a brief rest.
COMMON ERRORS
Lack of vertical height achieved before achieving the
tuck position
Poorly coordinated sequencing of the arm action relative
to the upward drive from the legs
