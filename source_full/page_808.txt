FIGURE 14.24 Layout for the 5-0-5 test.
29   ILLINOIS TEST
Equipment
Stopwatch or timing lights
Tape measure
Eight cones
Personnel
One tester/recorder
Procedure
1. Set up the cones on a course of 10 meters (11 yd) in length by 5 meters (5 yd) in width. Four
cones should be on the corners of the 10-meter × 5-meter square, with the additional four
placed in the center of the 5-meter width equally spaced along the 10-meter length. This will
create three aisles of cones in the order of two, four, and two through which the athlete will
run (figure 14.25).
2. Instruct the athlete to lie prone at the cone to the outside left of the full 10-meter length. The
athlete will be instructed to get up and run when the tester says “Go,” sprint to the cone 10
meters ahead, and then turn around to sprint back to the cones in the middle aisle.
