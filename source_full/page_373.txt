FIGURE 6.4 A graphical representation of the proposed physiological mechanisms of
altitude and heat acclimation training and their subsequent effect on aerobic endurance
exercise performance. Erythropoietin = EPO; nitric oxide = NO; red cell volume = RCV;
vascular endothelial growth factor = VEGF; hypoxia-inducible factor alpha = HIF-α; maximal
aerobic capacity = V̇ O2max; arteriovenous oxygen difference = a-vO2 diff. Solid lines
indicate well-established relationship and dashed lines indicate limited evidence supporting
the relationship.
