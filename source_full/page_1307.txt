  4   JUMP AND REACH
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Vertical
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm with reach at the top of the jump
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Explosively jump up and reach for an object or
target.
Downward movement: Ankles should be dorsiflexed in flight and
ready for an active flat-foot landing. Land in the starting position and
immediately repeat the jump.
Note: Emphasis is on vertical height with minimal delay between
jumps. Also, this drill should be performed with little horizontal
