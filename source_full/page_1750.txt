Other areas to consider related to storage include determining
where athletes will store personal items, such as coats, backpacks,
and water bottles, during training sessions. If no locker room is
available at the facility, designating an area for personal item storage
is important to avoid clutter. The use of cube storage, shelving, or
other functional storage solutions can help maintain organizational
flow of the weight room.
