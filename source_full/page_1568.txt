  4   STAIR STEPPER
Starting Position
Hold the handrails while stepping forward onto the pedals.
Place the whole foot in contact with each pedal.
Movement Phase
Begin stepping, using the handrails for support.
While maintaining an upright posture and with the legs
providing the force, take deep (4- to 8-in. [10-20 cm]) steps.
Keep the hips level and centered on the machine to avoid
swaying from side to side or step to step.
