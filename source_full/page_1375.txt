  45   SINGLE-ARM THROW
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Forward
Equipment: Medicine or plyometric ball weighing 1 to 5 pounds (0.5-
2.3 kg); wall or partner
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart; face the wall or partner approximately 10 feet (3 m)
away. Raise the ball in one hand to a position of 90 degrees of
shoulder abduction and 90 degrees of elbow flexion, with the arm
rotated so the forearm is perpendicular to the ground.
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement (i.e., cock
the arm by moving it slightly backward before the throw).
