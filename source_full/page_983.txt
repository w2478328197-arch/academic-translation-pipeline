Downward Movement Phase
Lower the dumbbell until the elbow is fully extended.
Keep the dumbbell in a neutral grip position.
Keep the torso and knees in the same position.
Repeat the upward and downward movement phases with the
other arm (alternate arms).
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
brachialis, biceps brachii, brachioradialis
VERBAL CUES
Curl up, thumbs to shoulders
Control the descent; imagine crushing something
between your biceps and forearm
COMMON ERRORS
Swinging the body or using a rocking motion instead of a
controlled motion
Not using a full ROM by not lowering the weight fully or
not curling the weight high enough
