VERBAL CUES
Initiate dip with hips, not knees
Drive from your heels

COMMON ERRORS
Pushing hips back during the dip
Dropping the elbows too much during the dip (as seen in
the photos on the previous page)
Shifting to the toes during the dip
Note: The upper arms should be pointing away from the body, not toward the floor, in
the starting position through the dip. The result is about 45 degrees of shoulder flexion
