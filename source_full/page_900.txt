Posterior of Upper Arm
  5   BEHIND-NECK STRETCH (CHICKEN
WING)
1. Standing or sitting, abduct the right shoulder and flex the
elbow.
2. Reach the right hand down toward the left scapula.
3. Grasp the right elbow with the left hand.
4. Pull the elbow behind the head with the left hand to increase
shoulder abduction.
5. Repeat on the other side.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
triceps brachii, latissimus dorsi
