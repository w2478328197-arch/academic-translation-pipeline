Lower Back
  8   SPINAL TWIST (PRETZEL)
1. Sitting with the legs straight and the upper body nearly vertical,
place the right foot to the left side of the left knee.
2. Place the back of the left elbow on the right side of the right
knee, which is now flexed.
3. Place the right palm on the floor 12 to 16 inches (30-41 cm)
behind the hips.
4. Push the right knee to the left with the left elbow while turning
the shoulders and head to the right as far as possible. Try to
look behind the back.
5. Repeat on the other side.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
internal oblique, external oblique, piriformis, erector
spinae
