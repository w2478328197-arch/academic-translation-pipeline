  15   DOUBLE-LEG ZIGZAG JUMP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Diagonal
Equipment: Place 8 to 12 hurdles 18 to 24 inches (46-61 cm) apart
in a zigzag pattern.
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart. Stand on the outside of the first hurdle. Elbows
should be flexed at 90 degrees and held at the sides of the body.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
