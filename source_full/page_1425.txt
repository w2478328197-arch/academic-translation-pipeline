Ankle in neutral position (avoid plantar flexed position). Preparation for

Early Support
Continued concentric hip extension. Minimizes the braking effect of the foot
strike
Brief concentric knee flexion followed by eccentric knee extension. Absorbs
the landing and facilitates power transfer from the hip to the ankle during
propulsion
Eccentric plantar flexion. Stores potential elastic energy for subsequent
release to facilitate greater propulsion during late support
Late Support
Eccentric hip flexion. Decelerates backward thigh rotation; rotates the trunk
in preparation for forward takeoff
Concentric knee extension. Propels the center of gravity forward
Concentric plantar flexion. Aids in propulsion
