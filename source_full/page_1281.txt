Program Length
Research has yet to determine an optimal plyometric training
program length. Currently, most programs range from 6 to 10 weeks
(2, 34); however, vertical jump height improves as quickly as four
weeks after the start of a plyometric training program (54). In
general, plyometric training should be prescribed similarly to
resistance training within a periodized plan. For those sports
requiring quick, powerful movements, it is beneficial to perform
plyometric exercises throughout the training cycle. The intensity and
volume of the selected drills should vary with the training status of
the athlete and the sport season.
