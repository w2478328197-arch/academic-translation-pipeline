Allow the wrists to extend and the hands to open so that the
fingertips hold the bar.
Upward Movement Phase
Raise the bar by flexing the fingers and then the wrists.
Flex the wrists as far as possible without moving the elbows or
forearms.
Do not jerk the shoulders backward or swing the bar upward.
Downward Movement Phase
Allow the wrists and fingers to slowly extend back to the
starting position.
Keep the torso and arms in the same position.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
flexor carpi ulnaris, flexor carpi radialis, palmaris longus
VERBAL CUES
Wrists off the edge of the bench
Curl only from the wrists
Full ROM
COMMON ERRORS
Not lowering the wrist fully or not curling up completely
Raising the forearms off the support surface during the
movement
