  3   ROWING MACHINE
Starting Position (Start)
Keep the back upright, not rounded, with a slight forward lean
from the hips.
Hold the head upright while looking straight ahead.
Extend the arms in front of the body and grab the rower handle
while flexing the hips and knees until the shins are
approximately vertical.
Movement Phase (Drive)
Extend the knees first followed by the hips before using the
arms to pull the handle toward the abdomen just below the rib
cage. As the knees near their end range of motion, tilt the
upper body backward by extending the hips before pulling with
the arms to complete the movement.
Adjust the air vent to increase (more air admitted) or decrease
(less air admitted) the resistance provided.
