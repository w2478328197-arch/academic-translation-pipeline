  34   LATERAL BOX JUMP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Vertical and slightly horizontal
Equipment: Plyometric box, 6 to 42 inches (15-107 cm) high
Starting position: Stand to one side of the plyometric box; get into a
comfortable, upright stance with feet hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Jump onto the top of the box using both legs.
Downward movement: Land on both feet in a half-squat position and
jump down, landing with an active flat-foot contact and optimal
ground contact to enable the jump to be repeated in the opposite
direction.
Note: If the athlete steps down rather than jumps down from the
box, the drill would be classified as a low-intensity ballistic exercise.
Also, this drill can be made into a low-intensity ballistic drill by
stepping down off the box and jumping up and laterally only.
