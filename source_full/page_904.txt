  9   SEMI-LEG STRADDLE
1. Sit with knees flexed 30 degrees to 50 degrees; let the legs
totally relax.
2. Point the knees outward; the sides of the knees may or may not
touch the floor.
3. Lean forward from the waist and reach forward with extended
arms.
Note: Flexing the knees and relaxing the legs decreases hamstring
involvement and increases lower back stretch.
