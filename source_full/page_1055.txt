Downward Movement Phase
Allow the dumbbells to descend slowly back to the starting
position.
Keep the torso and knees in the same position.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
deltoids
VERBAL CUES
Raise your arms to shoulder height
Imagine pouring water from pitchers
Lower slowly
COMMON ERRORS
Swinging the body to lift the weights instead of using
controlled shoulder movement
Raising the arms above shoulder level
Dropping the weights quickly instead of lowering them
under control
