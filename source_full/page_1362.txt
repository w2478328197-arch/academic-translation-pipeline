Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Step from the box.
Downward movement: Dorsiflex the ankles in preparation for an
active flat-foot landing with both feet. Have a partner point to the
right or left just before landing.
Upward movement: On landing, immediately sprint in the direction
determined by the partner.
Note: When stepping from the box, step straight out. Do not first
jump up or lower the center of gravity while stepping down, because
these adjustments will change the height from which the exercise is
performed. The time on the ground should be kept to a minimum.
COMMON ERRORS
The athlete prioritizing the sprint over landing
mechanics, leading to poor alignment on landing
The partner signaling which direction to sprint too late
for the athlete to prepare for landing and a reactionary
sprint; body lean and weight distribution between the
inside and outside of the feet are important
considerations for successful sprints from landing
