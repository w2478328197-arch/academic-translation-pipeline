  3   SUSPENSION TRAINER CHEST
PRESS
Starting Position
Assume a position away from the anchor point while grabbing
the handles of the suspension trainer in each hand.
With the elbows extended, move the hands and feet shoulder-
width apart while placing the body into a straight line, or plank
position.
Brace the trunk and slowly step back until there is tension on
the straps and the body is in an inclined position.
Movement
Initiate the movement by flexing the elbows and lowering the
chest, similar to a push-up.
Ending Position
At the end of the movement the chest should be between the
suspension trainer’s handles.
