Starting position: One leg is lifted to approximately 90 degrees of hip
and knee flexion with the ankle dorsiflexed.
Arm action: Reciprocal (as one leg is lifted, the opposite arm is
lifted)
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement on one leg.
Upward movement: Drive up and forward on one leg. The opposite
leg should remain in the starting flexed position until landing.
Downward movement: Land in the starting position on the same leg.
Immediately repeat the skip with the opposite leg.
COMMON ERRORS
Rotating at the hip and shoulders during the movement
(which should be in the sagittal plane only), detracting
from the velocity achieved
Coordination errors in the reciprocal arm and leg action,
which detract from the forward momentum achieved
