  20   PROGRESSIVE LATERAL JUMP
Intensity level: High
Direction: Diagonally lateral and vertical
Equipment: String tied at athlete’s eye level and loosely secured to
the ground at a 45-degree angle to the ground
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance on one side
of the string next to the anchor on the ground.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Movements:
1. Explosively jump diagonally (sideways and forward) over the
string using both arms to assist the movement.
