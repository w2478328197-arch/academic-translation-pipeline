Arm action: Using one arm, throw the ball to the wall or partner.
When the wall or partner returns the ball, catch the ball and return
to the starting position, allow the shoulder to externally rotate
slightly, and immediately repeat the throw.
Note: This drill may also be performed using a natural throwing
motion. Performing the action from a half-kneeling position may help
isolate the upper body action of the throwing motion.
COMMON ERRORS
Not achieving a stable throwing position due to lack of
thoracic mobility and overcompensations through
lumbar spine flexion
Not throwing from the shoulder but rotating through the
trunk (lumbar spine); this is likely to occur when the ball
is too heavy for the athlete
