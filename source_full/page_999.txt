Sit down and lean back on the seat in the five-point body
contact position.
Grasp the handles with a closed, pronated grip.
Align handles with the nipples. If necessary, adjust the seat
height to correctly position the handles.
All repetitions begin from this position.
Forward Movement Phase
Push the handles away from the chest to a fully extended elbow
position.
Do not arch the lower back or forcefully lock out the elbows.
Maintain the five-point body contact position.
Backward Movement Phase
Allow the handles to slowly move backward to the starting
position.
Maintain the five-point body contact position.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
pectoralis major, anterior deltoids, triceps brachii
VERBAL CUES
Back flat against the pad
Feet flat on the floor
Push the handles straight out
COMMON ERRORS
Arching the back off the pad while performing the
exercise
Shortening the ROM during the backward movement
phase
