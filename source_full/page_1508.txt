  11   EVASION DRILL
Task
Using a 10 × 10-yard (or -meter) box, the purpose of this open
drill from an attacking perspective is to create space, take
space, create separation, evade an opponent, and penetrate
defensive lines accurately and efficiently and entering a specific
end zone (e.g., 1v1 evasive drill with two gates). A range of
different COD actions, deceleration actions, or simply linear or
curvilinear speed actions could be performed to achieve the
objective. Conversely, from a defensive perspective, the aim is
to prevent the attacker from penetrating the defensive line and
entering the end zones, limiting space for the attacker,
potentially making a tag, or delaying the opponent. The
defender is encouraged to identify kinematic and postural cues
and anticipate and react to the opponent.
