  24   ONE-ARM DUMBBELL SNATCH
Starting Position
Straddle a dumbbell and place the feet between hip- and
shoulder-width apart with the toes pointed slightly outward.
Squat down with the hips lower than the shoulders; grasp the
dumbbell with a closed, pronated grip and the elbow fully
extended.
All repetitions begin with the back neutral or slightly arched,
scapulae depressed and retracted, and chest held up and out.
