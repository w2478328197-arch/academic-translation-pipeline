  6   STRAIGHT-LEG BOUND
This drill emphasizes rapid retraction of the lower limb, offering
similarity to the desired touchdown leg mechanics of maximum-
velocity sprinting and increasing hip angular velocity and foot
touchdown velocity.
Starting Position
Stand with the feet hip-width apart and the head and trunk
upright.
Movement Phase
After an initial upward and forward hop from one limb, while
maintaining a straight limb (extended knee and neutral ankle
position), drive the opposite hip forward and then rapidly retract
the limb to make ground contact on the forefoot before
rebounding and repeating with the opposite limb.
Emphasize the rapid retraction of the limb in preparation for
ground contact, and ensure that ground contact times remain
short and, ideally, under the hips.
The trunk should be maintained in an upright position (must
avoid leaning back), with the head up and eyes looking forward.
