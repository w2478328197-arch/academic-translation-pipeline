Progressions and Regressions
Multiplanar acceleration initiations to decelerations can be
further developed using generic and sport-specific stimuli. For
example, multiplanar acceleration initiation box drills can be
progressed whereby the athletes must react to an auditory or
visual stimulus when initiating their acceleration, such as a color
cone or auditory command (e.g., left, right, forward, rear left,
rear right) (figure a). The tasks can be further developed by
incorporating a sport-specific stimuli such as an opponent and
