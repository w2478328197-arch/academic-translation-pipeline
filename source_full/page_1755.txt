Aerobic Area
The aerobic area is where the cardiovascular training equipment is
grouped together. This equipment consists of stationary bicycles,
stair climbers, elliptical trainers, treadmills, rowers, and so on. A
general guideline is to allocate approximately 40 to 60 square feet
(3.7-5.6 m2) of floor space per piece of equipment (2). Specific floor
space requirements for aerobic equipment and the recommended
space between pieces of equipment are provided in table 25.1.
These requirements outline the amount of space needed for each
piece of equipment, along with the required amount of space to
allow for room to move and prevent accidents caused by falling into
equipment. Within aerobic training areas it is important that there is
adequate spacing around and behind all continuous-motion exercise
equipment (e.g., treadmills, elliptical trainers, bikes, ski trainers, and
rowers) (2).
