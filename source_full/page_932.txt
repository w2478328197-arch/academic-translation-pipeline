  35   HEEL-TO-TOE WALK
1. Stand erect with the feet parallel to each other and shoulder-
width apart.
2. Take a small step forward with the right leg; place the heel of
the right foot on the ground first and then continue to dorsiflex
the foot.
3. Immediately roll forward and rise up as high as possible onto
the ball of the right foot.
4. Swing the left leg forward in order to take another small step.
5. Repeat with the left leg, progressing forward with each step.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
gastrocnemius, soleus, anterior tibialis
COMMON ERROR
932
