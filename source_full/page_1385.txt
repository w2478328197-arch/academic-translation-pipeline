  50   MEDICINE BALL SLAM FROM
SINGLE-LEG BALANCE
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Downward
Equipment: Medicine or plyometric ball weighing 10 to 30 pounds
(5-14 kg)
Starting position: Start in a single leg Romanian deadlift position,
with the trunk parallel to the ground and the hips hinged and level
with one leg extended straight behind the body. The stance foot
should be flat on the ground with weight distributed toward the heel.
The shoulders should be flexed so the straight arms are holding the
medicine ball level with the head.
Preparatory movement: Initiate a small countermovement by flexing
the shoulders.
