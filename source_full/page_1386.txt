Arm action: Keeping the arms straight, slam the ball directly down
into the ground, maintaining a stable trunk and hip position. Catch
the rebound and repeat the movement, then repeat on the
contralateral leg.
Note: With the shoulder position, small increases in load can make a
big impact on drill intensity. This drill also can be adapted to a chest-
dominant pattern by starting with the ball held at the chest and
slammed into the ground from there.
COMMON ERRORS
Not achieving a stable and balanced position at the hips
to form a stable platform from which to throw the ball
Hyperextending into the lumbar spine to achieve
sufficient power to throw the ball to the ground rather
than throwing from the shoulder
