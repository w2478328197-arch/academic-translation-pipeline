Backpedal
Starting Stance
Stand in a split stance, ensuring stability.
Lower the center of mass by sinking the hips.
Flex the front hip and knee to approximately 45 degrees.
Flex the back knee to approximately 90 degrees and the hips to around 60
degrees.
The front foot should be flat (firm base of support), while the rear foot heel
should be raised. Distribute weight toward the balls of both feet.
Keep the back flat.
Lean the torso, with the shoulders over the front knee.
The head is up looking forward (or toward the opponent; visual scanning).
Relax the arms and hang them straight ahead of the hips.
