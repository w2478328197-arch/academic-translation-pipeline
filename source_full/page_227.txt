CHAPTER 4
ENDOCRINE RESPONSES TO
RESISTANCE EXERCISE AND
TRAINING
William J. Kraemer, PhD, Jakob L. Vingren, PhD,
and Disa L. Hatfield, PhD
After completing this chapter, you will be able to
identify basic concepts of endocrinology and explain the
endocrine system’s role in human physiology;
describe the major hormones, including their sources, target
tissues, and physiological effects;
analyze and explain the physiological roles of anabolic and
catabolic hormones in the body’s metabolism and response to
stress;
examine and describe how resistance exercise and training
elicit specific hormonal responses, differentiating between
various types of resistance exercises;
evaluate the impact of different resistance exercise workouts
on hormonal responses, using scientific evidence to support an
analysis; and
use an understanding of human endocrine responses to
develop effective workouts and training programs that optimize
