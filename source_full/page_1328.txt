  18   LATERAL BARRIER JUMP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Lateral and vertical
Equipment: A barrier such as a cone or hurdle
Starting position: With the barrier to one side, get into a
comfortable, upright stance with feet hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Leaning out of the countermovement in the
direction of travel, use a powerful triple extension to jump over the
barrier as tall as possible with both legs, using necessary hip and
