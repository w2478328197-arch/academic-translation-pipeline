Adverse Effects
Synephrine has been shown to stimulate peripheral α-1 receptors,
resulting in vasoconstriction and elevations in blood pressure (31).
However, other research has shown that when citrus aurantium is
ingested alone, no effect on blood pressure is seen (103), but in
combination with other herbal products it may cause significant
elevations in systolic blood pressure (103). It should be noted that
synephrine is currently on the National Collegiate Athletic
Association’s banned list of performance-enhancing drugs.
