Upper Back
  6   CROSS ARM IN FRONT OF CHEST
1. Stand or sit with the left elbow slightly flexed (15-30 degrees)
and the arm across the body (i.e., shoulder in horizontal
adduction).
2. Grasp the upper arm just above the elbow, placing the right
hand on the posterior side of the upper arm.
3. Pull the left arm across the chest (toward the right) with the
right hand.
4. Repeat on the other side.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
posterior deltoid, rhomboids, middle trapezius
