Similarly, as the training environment changes (e.g., from cool to hot

intensity in addition to heart rate, which will be elevated by the
increased physiologic demands induced by the environmental
conditions. In conjunction with these points, progression of training
frequency, intensity, and duration, along with recovery, should be
monitored at all times to ensure appropriate periods of loading and
unloading are applied, and signs or symptoms of overreaching or
overtraining are detected early. More detailed information on these
