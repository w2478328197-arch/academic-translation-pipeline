The first half of this chapter summarizes the fundamental
techniques involved in properly performing and spotting exercises
and using a weight belt during lifting. The last section provides
checklists and photographs depicting proper resistance training
exercise and spotting techniques. It is assumed that the reader is
familiar with these exercises. Therefore, the techniques presented
are simply guidelines that represent the most commonly accepted
method of performing the exercises. No attempt has been made to
describe all variations for properly executing and spotting the
exercises included in this chapter.
