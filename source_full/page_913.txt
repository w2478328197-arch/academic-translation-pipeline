Posterior of Thigh
  18   SITTING TOE TOUCH
1. Sit with the upper body nearly vertical and legs straight.
2. Lean forward using hip flexion and grasp the toes with each
hand. Slightly pull the toes toward the upper body and pull the
chest toward the legs. If you have limited flexibility, try to grasp
the ankles.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
hamstrings, erector spinae, gastrocnemius
