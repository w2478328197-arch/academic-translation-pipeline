  5   ELLIPTICAL TRAINER
Starting Position
Facing the center console of the elliptical, place one foot on
each pedal.
While standing upright and looking forward, grasp the handrails
with the torso upright and evenly balanced directly over the
hips, head held high, and shoulders relaxed (but not rounded).
Movement Phase
Begin pedaling forward, moving the arms and legs in a
reciprocating fashion.
