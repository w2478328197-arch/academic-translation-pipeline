  11   HAMMER CURL
Starting Position
Grasp two dumbbells using a closed, neutral grip.
Stand erect with the feet shoulder-width apart and the knees
slightly flexed.
Position the dumbbells alongside the thighs with the elbows
fully extended.
All repetitions begin from this position.
Upward Movement Phase
Keeping the dumbbell in a neutral grip, flex the elbow of one
arm until the dumbbell is near the anterior deltoid. The other
arm should be kept stationary at the side of the thigh.
Keep the torso erect and the upper arm stationary.
Do not jerk the body or swing the dumbbell upward.
