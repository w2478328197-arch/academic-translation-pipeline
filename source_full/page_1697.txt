Factors Contributing to Overreaching
and Overtraining
The first step in understanding overtraining and overreaching is
identifying the balance between stress and recovery (53). Stress
from a strength and conditioning program must be countered with
adequate recovery to permit long-term progression (figure 24.8).
The inability to adequately recover will undoubtedly result in an
overtraining scenario. Likewise, an inadequate training load will likely
produce suboptimal performance. The challenge for the strength and
conditioning professional is to find the appropriate balance to
maximize the long-term performance adaptations.
