  7   ARMS STRAIGHT UP ABOVE HEAD
(PILLAR)
1. Stand with the arms in front of the torso, fingers interlocked
with palms facing out.
2. Slowly straighten the arms above the head with the palms up.
3. Continue to reach upward with the hands and arms.
4. While continuing to reach upward, slowly reach slightly
backward.
MUSCLE AFFECTED
latissimus dorsi
