Keep the thumbs crossed and hands close to—but not touching
—the bar as it ascends.
Slightly extend the knees, hips, and torso and keep the back
neutral when following the bar.
At the end of the set, move sideways in unison with the athlete
back to the rack.
Simultaneously grasp the bar and assist with balancing the bar
as it is placed back on the supports.
Release the bar smoothly.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
gluteus maximus, semimembranosus, semitendinosus,
biceps femoris, vastus lateralis, vastus intermedius, vastus
medialis, rectus femoris
VERBAL CUES
Create a shelf with your shoulders
Keep your elbows high
Sit between your heels
COMMON ERRORS
Allowing the arms to relax or the elbows to drop down
and backward
Excessive forward lean or raising with the hips first
