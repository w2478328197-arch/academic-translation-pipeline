Upward Movement Phase: Spotter
Keep the hands in the alternated grip position close to—but not
touching—the bar as it ascends.
Slightly extend the knees, hips, and torso and keep the back
neutral when following the bar.
At the athlete’s signal, grasp the bar with an alternated grip
inside the athlete’s hands.
Guide the bar back onto the supports.
Keep a grip on the bar until it is racked.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
pectoralis major, anterior deltoids, triceps brachii
VERBAL CUES
Push yourself away from the bar
Try to bend the bar; touch and go
COMMON ERRORS
Bouncing the bar off the chest during the upward
movement phase
Lifting the hips off the bench
Not wrapping the thumbs around the barbell
