After completing this chapter, you will be able to
identify the members of the allied health team and their
responsibilities during the rehabilitation and reconditioning of
injured athletes;
recognize the types of injuries and medical conditions that
athletes sustain;
comprehend the timing and events of tissue healing;
understand the goals of each tissue healing phase; and
describe the role of the strength and conditioning professional
during injury rehabilitation and reconditioning.
