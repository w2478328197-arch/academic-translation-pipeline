Regression and Progression
A regression of the drill includes a B-march or -walk, which
involves the same movements but at a slower pace to establish
the correct leg recovery and touchdown mechanics. A
progression from the B-skip involves a running form
emphasizing the leg recovery to touchdown action and
overhead variations to focus on trunk stability and lumbopelvic
control.
