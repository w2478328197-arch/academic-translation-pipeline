Abdomen
  1   BENT-KNEE SIT-UP
Starting Position
Lie in a supine position on a floor mat.
Flex the knees to bring the heels near the buttocks.
Fold the arms across the chest or abdomen.
All repetitions begin from this position.
Upward Movement Phase
Flex the neck to move the chin toward the chest.
Keeping the feet, buttocks, and lower back neutral and
stationary on the mat, curl the torso toward the thighs until the
upper back is off the mat.
