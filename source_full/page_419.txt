Advancing age is also associated with a loss of muscle mass,
strength, and function (19, 38). The term sarcopenia refers to a
progressive and generalized skeletal muscle disorder that is
associated with an increased likelihood of adverse outcomes
including falls, fractures, physical disability, and mortality (42).
