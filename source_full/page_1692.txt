Functional Overreaching
Many training programs purposely include micro- or mesocycles of
greater training volumes or intensities as part of the long-term plan.
Examples of these include preseason training camps for many sports
that include two or three training sessions a day or general
preparation training blocks in the strength and conditioning program.
