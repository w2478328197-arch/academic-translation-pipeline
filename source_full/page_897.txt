  2   FLEXION AND EXTENSION
1. Standing or sitting with head and neck upright, flex the neck by
tucking the chin toward the chest.
2. If the chin touches the chest, try to touch the chin lower on the
chest.
3. Extend the neck by trying to come as close as possible to
touching the head to the back.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
sternocleidomastoid, suboccipitals, splenae
