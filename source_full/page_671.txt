athletes desire. In a classic study on the dose–response curve of
anabolic steroids, Forbes (68) demonstrated that the total dose of
anabolic steroids has a logarithmic relationship to increases in lean
body mass; low doses produce only slight effects, but there is a
progressive augmentation of lean body mass with increasingly larger
doses. These results reinforce the athlete’s philosophy that if a low
dose is effective, more must be better.
