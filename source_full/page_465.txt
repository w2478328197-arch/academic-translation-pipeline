After completing this chapter, you will be able to
understand the psychological constructs of arousal, motivation,
focus, and confidence and be able to ascertain their impact on
physical performance;
comprehend terms relevant to psychological areas of concern,
such as anxiety, attention, and the ideal performance state;
understand signs and symptoms of mental health issues,
predisposing factors to injury, psychological recovery,
techniques to handle fear of reinjury, introduction of
rehabilitation-type strength and conditioning, performance
psychology concepts, arousal management concepts such as
self-efficacy, imagery, goal setting, progressive muscle
relaxation, and centering;
