CHAPTER 15
PERFORMANCE
PREPARATION, MOBILITY,
AND FLEXIBILITY
Ian Jeffreys, PhD
After completing this chapter, you will be able to
identify the components and benefits of a preperformance
preparation routine;
structure effective preperformance preparation routines (warm-
ups);
identify the factors that affect flexibility;
use flexibility exercises that take advantage of proprioceptive
neuromuscular facilitation; and
select and apply appropriate static and dynamic stretching
methods.
