One of the biggest challenges when training an individual or a
team is how to get the most from training. If working with athletes
training for maximum performance, this means implementing a
training program that will optimize performance for their sport. If
working in a tactical environment (e.g., military, law enforcement,
fire fighter, first responder), the goal again is to optimize
performance for the tasks to be performed. Even for those in the
general population who are hoping to optimize their health and
fitness, it is sometimes desirable to get the maximum effects from
the training program to satisfy their needs. In each of these
scenarios, the person designing the training program will most likely
