  18   PEC DECK (MACHINE)
Starting Position
Sit down and lean back on the seat in the five-point body
contact position. (Note: Some back pads are not long enough to
allow the back of the head to be in contact with the bench.)
Grasp the handles with a closed, neutral grip.
Align the handles with the midchest so that the arms are
parallel to the floor. If necessary, adjust the seat height to
correctly position the handles.
All repetitions begin from this position.
Forward Movement Phase
Pull the handles toward each other while maintaining a slight
flex in the elbow, until the fingers touch in front of the body.
