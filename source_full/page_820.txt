36   BIOELECTRICAL IMPEDANCE (BIA)
Equipment
BIA device
Personnel
One recorder
Procedure
1. Program the device with the athlete’s sex, weight, and height.
2. Have the athlete either hold the electrodes in the hands or stand barefoot on the electrodes
depending on the type of device.
3. Press the start button and wait the specified duration of time for that device (usually under 5
seconds).
4. From the measuring device, obtain and record the body composition measurements provided.
5. This means of evaluating body composition is best done under similar circumstances. To most
effectively monitor body composition changes, it is recommended that testing be done first
thing in the morning before the athlete has exercised, before food or water consumption, and
after having voided.
Note: Descriptive data for percent body fat are presented in tables 14.22 through 14.25 and table
14.37 near the end of the chapter.
