  26   DROP STEP TO LATERAL BOUND
AND RETURN
Intensity level: High
Direction: Lateral and vertical
Equipment: Plyometric box, 6 to 18 inches (15-46 cm) high
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart on the plyometric box; toes should be near the edge
of the box.
Arm action: None
Preparatory movement: Step from the box with the right foot, ankles
dorsiflexed.
Downward movement: Land on the ground with active flat foot
contact in the right foot.
Upward movement: Immediately push up and sideways toward the
