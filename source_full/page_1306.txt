Intensity level: Low
Direction: Vertical
Starting position: Get into a squat position (thighs slightly above
parallel with the ground) with feet hip- to shoulder-width apart.
Interlock the fingers and place the hands behind the head.
Arm action: None
Preparatory movement: None
Upward movement: Explosively jump up to a maximum height.
Extend the body as tall as possible.
Downward movement: Ankles should be dorsiflexed in flight and
ready for flat foot landing. (Note: The second photo does not yet
show the feet in the needed position for landing.) Land in the squat
position, and immediately repeat the jump.
COMMON ERRORS
Landing and immediately jumping again before resetting
to achieve squat depth for the subsequent jump
Lack of hip and knee extension, meaning the body is not
fully extended in flight
