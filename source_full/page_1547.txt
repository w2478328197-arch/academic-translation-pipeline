This program has a swim focus; the race swim distance is 2.4 miles (3.9 km).
Comments
Frequency. Because the interval and Fartlek workouts are stressful, these training
sessions should be spread out during the week to allow recovery between sessions.
Duration. The intervals are work bouts of 2 minutes with ≤2-minute passive recovery
between repetitions.
Repetitions. The intervals are 6 to 10 reps × 2 minutes.
Intensity. The athlete should swim at an intensity (pace) at or above 80% V̇ O2max
when completing the work portions of the interval session. The aerobic swims should
be at zone 1 on the three-zone intensity scale (figure 21.1 on page 701) or zone 2 on
the five-zone intensity scale (table 21.1 on page 697).
Preseason
During the preseason, the athlete should focus on progressively
increasing training intensity, maintaining or reducing duration, and
incorporating all types of aerobic training stimuli into the program.
The strengths and weaknesses of the individual athlete should
determine the amount and frequency of each type of training.
