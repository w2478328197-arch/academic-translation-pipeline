Calves
  12   STANDING CALF (HEEL) RAISE
(MACHINE)
Starting Position
Position the body evenly under the shoulder pads.
Grasp the handles and place the balls of the feet on the nearest
edge of the step with the legs and feet hip-width apart and
parallel to each other.
Stand erect with the knees fully extended but not forcefully
locked out.
Allow the heels to lower to a comfortable, stretched position.
All repetitions begin from this position.
