  54   SEATED ROTATIONAL MEDICINE
BALL THROW
Intensity level: Medium
Equipment: Medicine or plyometric ball weighing 5 to 30 pounds (2-
14 kg); a wall or partner
Starting position: Sit on the ground with the trunk at an
approximately 45-degree angle. The partner should stand to the side
with the medicine ball.
Preparatory movement: The partner throws the ball to the athlete’s
outstretched hands.
