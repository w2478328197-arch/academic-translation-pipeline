Situational Factors
In addition to personal factors, variability in environmental factors
can influence how athletes view their injury and their return to sport,
including the following:
Sport type. Is it individual or team based? Is there a high risk
of reinjury based on the sport?
Level of competition. Is this a high school athlete or a
professional athlete?
Social support. Is the athlete surrounded by friends, family,
and teammates for emotional and social support? Social
support helps one learn that they are valued and supported
regardless of athletic performance or the identity as a
competitive athlete (63).
Sports medicine team. Does the athlete have proper access to
a professional rehabilitative team?
