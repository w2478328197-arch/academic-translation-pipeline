  37   GIRTH MEASUREMENTS
Equipment
Flexible, spring-loaded tape measure (e.g., a Gulick tape)
Personnel
One tester, one recorder
Procedure
1. Position the athlete in a relaxed anatomical position for each measurement (unless otherwise
indicated for a particular measurement).
2. Measure the following sites (81); see figure 14.31:
Chest—at nipple level in males and at maximum circumference (above the breasts) in
females
Right upper arm—at the point of maximal circumference with the elbow fully extended,
palm up, and arm abducted to parallel with the floor
Right forearm—at the point of maximal circumference with the elbow fully extended, palm
up, and arm abducted to parallel with the floor
Waist (abdomen)—at the level of the umbilicus
Hips (buttocks)—at the maximal protrusion of the buttocks with the heels together
Right thigh—at the point of maximal circumference, usually just below the buttocks
Right calf—at the point of maximal circumference between the knee and ankle
