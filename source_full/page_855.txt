Flexibility training, on the other hand, aims to increase the range of
motion around a joint, normally through the use of different forms of
stretching. The first part of this chapter looks at the aims of a warm-
up and suggests appropriate structures and protocols for designing
effective warm-ups, and the second part discusses factors that affect
flexibility and the use of different stretching protocols to enhance
flexibility.
