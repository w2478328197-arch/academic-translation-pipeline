Overtraining Syndrome Defined
When the ECSS-ACSM Task Force defined overtraining (88, 89), they
specifically stated that the OTS was a combination of “related
physiological 
and 
psychological 
signs 
and 
symptoms 
of
maladaptation,” some of which may not be part of the training
program. As such, the use of the term syndrome indicates the
complex nature of the condition and the fact that the symptoms are
associated with each other.
