LOWER BODY PLYOMETRIC DRILLS
Jumps in Place
  1   POGO JUMP
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Vertical
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart.
Arm action: None or double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a slight countermovement.
Upward movement: Jump up, with primary motion at the ankle joint.
Downward movement: Land with an active flat foot contact in the
starting position, with hips and knees stiff, and immediately repeat.
Note: This drill should be performed with little horizontal (forward or
backward) or lateral movement.
COMMON ERRORS
Flexing the hips and knees on landing to increase vertical
force, which increases ground contact time and changes
the nature of the ankle-dominant exercise
Not dorsiflexing the ankle in flight; this makes athletes
land on their toes, which changes the nature of the
ground contact
