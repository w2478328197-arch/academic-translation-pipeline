FIGURE 14.19 Setup for the Yo-Yo intermittent recovery test.
   23  MAXIMAL AEROBIC SPEED TEST
Equipment
Cones
A tape measure at least 30 meters (33 yd) long
Audio software specifically for the test
Method of broadcasting the audio files (e.g., wireless speakers)
Recording sheet
Indoor or outdoor running track (at least 200 m [219 yd])
Personnel
One tester/recorder
Procedure
1. Marker cones are placed at 25-meter (27 yd) intervals around the running track.
2. The initial speed of the test is set between 8 and 12 km/h (5-7 mph) depending on the fitness
level of the athlete. It is generally recommended that athletes start at 10 km/h (6 mph).
3. The speed is then increased by 1 km/h (0.6 mph) every 2 minutes until the athlete cannot
maintain the speed.
