  10   COBRA STRETCH
1. Lie prone on the floor or mat.
2. Place the hands on the floor in a push-up position just below
the shoulders with the elbows flexed and held close to the body.
3. Keep the legs extended with the top of the toes or feet resting
on the floor.
4. Lift the head and chest while pressing down through the hands
to extend the spine (arch the back).
5. Be sure to keep the pelvis and lower body in contact with the
floor while looking forward. The result is only a partial extension
of the elbows unless the athlete is extremely flexible.
6. Hold the position while breathing slowly and deeply.
7. Slowly lower back to the floor to return to the beginning
position.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
rectus abdominis, pectoralis major, iliopsoas, erector
spinae
