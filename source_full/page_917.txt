  23   STEP STRETCH
1. Place the ball of one foot on the edge of a step or board 3 to 4
inches (8-10 cm) high, with the other foot flat on the step.
2. With straight legs, lower the heel of the foot on the edge of the
step as far as possible.
3. Repeat on the other side.
Note: To stretch the Achilles tendon, complete the same stretch with
10 degrees of knee flexion.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
gastrocnemius, soleus, Achilles tendon
