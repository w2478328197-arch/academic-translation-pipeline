Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement in the hips,
knees, and ankles as well as the arms (i.e., cocking the arms by
moving them slightly backward before the throw).
Arm action: Extend the legs forcefully, and transfer the power to
both arms, throwing the ball to the wall or partner by extending the
elbows. When the wall or partner returns the ball, catch it, return to
the starting position, and immediately repeat the movement.
COMMON ERRORS
Not initiating the movement from the legs, reducing the
impulse to the ball
Not coordinating or sequencing the transfer of power
from the lower body to the upper body, reducing the
impulse to the ball
