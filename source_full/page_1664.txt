lower extremity exercises to ensure that the involved right lower
extremity is protected. Exercises targeting the uninvolved limb can
improve muscle strength in the involved limb (55, 56). Thus it is
important for injured athletes to continue to perform strength and
conditioning exercises for the uninvolved body regions.
