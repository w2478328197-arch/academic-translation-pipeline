Macronutrient Guidelines
Protein
Choose a variety of protein foods, from both animal and plant sources, which
include seafood, lean meat and poultry, eggs, beans, peas and lentils, soy
products, nuts, and seeds.
Increase the amount and variety of seafood consumed by choosing seafood
in place of some meat and poultry.
Choose nuts, seeds, and soy products in place of some meat and poultry.
Aerobic endurance athletes should consume 1.2 to 2.0 grams of protein per
kilogram body weight per day.
