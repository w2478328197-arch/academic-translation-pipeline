The concept behind this proposed model is that an athlete may see
greater improvements in top speed if force production can occur at
the ideal rate and moment through an investment in acceleration
development and optimization (62). An alternative approach for
track sprinters (100-200 m) may involve sequencing training from
general to specific preparation starting with an aerobic foundation to
developing anaerobic capacity (short speed–endurance), to speed–
endurance (long speed–endurance) culminating in speed (inclusive
of acceleration and maximum velocity) before the main competition
(14, 46).
