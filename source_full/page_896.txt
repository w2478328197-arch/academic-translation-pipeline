Guidelines and Precautions for Static
Stretching
Get into a position that facilitates relaxation.
Move to the point in the ROM where you experience a sensation of mild
discomfort. If performing partner-assisted PNF stretching, communicate
clearly with your partner.
Hold stretches for 15 to 30 seconds.
Repeat unilateral stretches on both sides.
Decrease stretch intensity if you experience pain, radiating symptoms, or loss
of sensation.
Use caution when stretching a hypermobile joint.
Avoid combination movements that involve the spine (e.g., extension and
lateral flexion).
Stabilizing muscles should be active to protect other joints and prevent
unwanted movements.
