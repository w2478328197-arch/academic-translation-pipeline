Keep the hands in the alternated grip position close to—but not
touching—the bar as it descends.
Keep the knees slightly flexed and the back neutral when

Upward Movement Phase: Athlete
Push the bar upward until the elbows are fully extended.
Extend the neck slightly to allow the bar to pass by the face as
it is raised.
Keep the wrists stiff and the forearms parallel to each other.
Maintain the five-point body contact position.
Do not arch the back or rise off the seat.
At the end of the set, signal the spotter for assistance in racking
the bar.
Keep a grip on the bar until it is racked.
