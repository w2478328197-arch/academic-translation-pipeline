Shoulders and Chest
  3   STRAIGHT ARMS BEHIND BACK
1. Standing, place both arms behind the back.
2. Interlock fingers with palms facing each other.
3. Straighten the elbows fully.
4. Slowly raise the arms, keeping the elbows straight.
5. Keep the head upright and neck relaxed.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
anterior deltoid, pectoralis major
