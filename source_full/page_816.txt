5. Note that there are no universally accepted norms for body composition. When strength and
conditioning professionals assess an athlete’s body composition, they must account for a
standard error of the estimate (SEE) and report a range of percentages that the athlete falls
into. Note that the minimum SEE for population-specific skinfold equations is ±3% to ±5%.
Therefore, if a 25-year-old male athlete’s body fat is measured at 24%, there is a minimum of
a 6% range (21%-27%).
