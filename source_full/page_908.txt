  13   FIGURE FOUR STRETCH
1. Lie supine on the floor.
2. Flex and externally rotate the left hip to cross the left ankle over
the right thigh.
3. Flex the right hip and pull it toward the chest until a
comfortable stretch is felt.
4. Hold and then release.
5. Repeat on the other side.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
gluteus medius, piriformis
