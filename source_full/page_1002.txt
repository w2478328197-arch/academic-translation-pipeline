Forearms
  19   WRIST CURL
Starting Position
Sit on the end of a bench.
Grasp the bar with a closed, supinated grip about hip- to
shoulder-width apart.
Position the feet and legs parallel to each other with the toes
pointing straight ahead.
Lean the torso forward and place the elbows and forearms on
the top of the thighs.
Move the wrists forward until they extend slightly beyond the
patellae.
