This chapter is designed to provide the strength and conditioning
professional with a good working knowledge of the scientific
principles 
of 
aerobic 
endurance 
training 
and 
conditioning.
Specifically, this chapter includes information about the factors
related to performance, aerobic endurance training program design
variables, and various types of training programs. Additional
discussion focuses on sport season training and special issues
related to aerobic endurance training including concurrent training
(i.e., combining resistance and aerobic endurance training in one
training program) and heat acclimation. Because it would be
exhaustive to summarize all the relevant information about training
for all possible aerobic endurance sports, only foundational aerobic
endurance training topics are presented, with specific examples as
they relate to running, cycling, and swimming.
