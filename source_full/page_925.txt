desired number of repetitions on the same leg or alternate legs
for the desired number of repetitions.

quadriceps, hip adductors, hip abductors, gluteus

COMMON ERRORS
Taking a step that is too small or too long
Losing the feet alignment
Not maintaining postural alignment
