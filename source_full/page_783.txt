4. If using an instrumented device, record absolute force of eccentric hamstring force, typically
measured in newtons (N). Ratio or allometric scaling of peak force, as discussed earlier in the
chapter, may also be considered with Nordic hamstring force outputs to account for variations
in body mass and length on the peak force measurements. Other metrics such as torque in
newton-meters (Nm) or the percentage of asymmetry present between left and right
hamstrings may also be recorded if desired. If using videography alone, the break point angle
(the angle at which the external knee flexor moment can no longer be resisted and the athlete
loses active control of downward motion) can be reported as a moderate proxy of eccentric
hamstring strength.
