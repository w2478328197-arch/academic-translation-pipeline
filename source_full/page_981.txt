Keep the torso and knees in the same position.
Do not bounce the bar on the thighs between repetitions.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
biceps brachii, brachialis, brachioradialis
VERBAL CUES
Pin your elbows to your sides
Curl through the full range
Control the descent
COMMON ERRORS
Jerking the upper body, shrugging the shoulders,
extending the torso, extending the knees, swinging the
bar, or rising on the toes to help raise the bar
Keeping the elbows partially flexed at the end of the
downward movement phase (shortening the ROM)
