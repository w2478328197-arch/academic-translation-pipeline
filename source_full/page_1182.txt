Downward Movement Phase
Slowly allow the dumbbell to lower to the shoulder, then the
thigh, and finally to the floor between the feet using a squatting
movement.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
rectus femoris, vastus medialis, vastus lateralis, vastus
intermedius, gluteus maximus, semimembranosus,
semitendinosus, biceps femoris, deltoids, trapezius
KEY TERMS
accentuated eccentric loading (AEL)
accommodating resistance
alternative modes
anatomical core
anterior cruciate ligament (ACL)
augmented eccentric loading
