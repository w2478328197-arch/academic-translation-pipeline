  14   SIDE BEND WITH STRAIGHT ARMS
1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Interlace the fingers with the palms away from the torso and
facing outward.
3. Reach upward with straight arms.
4. Keeping the arms straight, lean from the waist to the left side.
Do not flex the knees.
5. Repeat on the other side.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
external oblique, latissimus dorsi, serratus anterior
