  16   LUNGE WITH SPINAL TWIST
1. Assume a lunge position with the right leg forward.
2. Place the left hand on the floor directly parallel to the right foot.
3. Rotate the body, extending the right arm toward the ceiling,
and hold.
4. Repeat on the other side.
