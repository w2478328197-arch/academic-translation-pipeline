athletes maintain their training in the days after heat acclimation to
assist retaining their thermoregulatory and physiological adaptations
(128).
It has also been reported that the heat acclimation adaptations
may remain longer for those athletes acclimatized in dry, hot
environments compared to humid, hot environments (121).
