COMMON ERRORS
Changing the orientation of the hips (i.e., rotate in the
transverse plane) during takeoff, which increases the
ground contact time on the subsequent jump
Not increasing ground contact time and hip and knee
flexion through the sequence, meaning insufficient force
is produced as the required jump height increases
