  27   QUADRUPED REACH AND ROTATE
1. Assume a quadruped position.
2. Raise the left hand and extend the hand under the body,
reaching as far to the right as possible.
3. Reverse the action, pulling the left hand back to the beginning
position before pulling the arm back and turning the body away
from the support arm.
4. Keep the lumbar spine in a neutral position so that the action
occurs mainly in the thoracic spine.
5. Carry out the action in a slow and controlled manner.
6. Repeat on the other side.
