The training required to improve agility becomes clearer as one
begins to understand the various demands required for COD and
agility with respect to neurophysiological factors, including the SSC,
eccentric muscle action neuromuscular training, and implications of
the length of time or the demand of the COD maneuver on the SMM.
Further, neurophysiological requirements of agility performance
extend beyond physical requirements to perceptual-cognitive
requirements that are specific to the tactical situation.
