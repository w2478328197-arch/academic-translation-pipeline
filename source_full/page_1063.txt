Catch (for the Push Press)
After the hips and knees are fully extended and the bar is
overhead from the drive phase, press it up the rest of the way
until the elbows are fully extended.
In this position the torso is erect, the head is in a neutral
position, the feet are flat on the floor, and the bar is slightly
over or behind the ears.
Downward Movement Phase
Lower the bar by gradually reducing the muscular tension of the
arms to allow a controlled descent of the bar to the shoulders.
Simultaneously flex the hips and knees to cushion the impact of
the bar on the shoulders.
At the end of the set, step toward the rack and place the bar in
the supports.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
gluteus maximus, semimembranosus, semitendinosus,
