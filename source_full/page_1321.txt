  14   MULTIPLE HORIZONTAL LOW
HURDLE JUMP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Horizontal and vertical
Starting position: Get into a comfortable upright stance with feet
hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Jump as far forward as possible and over the
first barrier. Coach the athlete to jump “upward and outward” and
“get as tall as possible over the hurdle” rather than tuck jump to
clear and not extend the body fully.
