  28   LUNGE WALK
1. Stand erect with the feet parallel to each other and shoulder-
width apart.
2. Take an exaggerated step directly forward with the left leg,
planting the left foot flat on the floor pointing straight ahead.
3. Allow the left hip and knee to slowly flex, keeping the left knee
directly over the left foot.
4. Slightly flex the right knee and lower it until it is 1 to 2 inches
(3-5 cm) above the floor; the right foot should be pointed
straight ahead.
