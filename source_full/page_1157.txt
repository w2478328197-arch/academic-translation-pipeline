  14   FORWARD SLED PUSH AND PULL
Starting Position (Push)
Stand behind the sled and position the hands on the vertical
bars of the prowler at a comfortable height, typically
somewhere between hip and knee height in a standing position.
(Note: The lower the hands are positioned on the prowler, the
more likely this will result in a horizontal trunk and shin
position, which will increase the ability to apply propulsive
horizontal forces and accelerate the sled.)
Keep the hands closer to the shoulders and elbows near the
hips (shown) or extend the arms forward in front of the body
(not shown), depending on personal preference.
