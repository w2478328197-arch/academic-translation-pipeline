Conclusion
Nutrition plays an important role in strength and conditioning.
Adequate hydration and electrolytes; appropriate energy intake; and
adequate protein, carbohydrate, fat, vitamin, and mineral intakes
allow athletes to reap maximal benefits from training. A general
understanding of nutrition principles and applications is essential for
strength and conditioning professionals so that they can help
athletes sort through nutrition misinformation and provide sound
guidelines that athletes can use to improve their diets.
