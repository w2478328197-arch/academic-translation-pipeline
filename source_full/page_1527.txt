Target Heart Rate Calculations
Karvonen Method
Formula
Age-predicted maximum heart rate (APMHR) = 220 - age
Heart rate reserve (HRR) = APMHR - resting heart rate (RHR)
Target heart rate (THR) = (HRR × exercise intensity) + RHR
To determine the target heart rate range (THRR), calculate THR using both the
prescribed minimum and maximum intensity values.
Example
A 30-year-old athlete with an RHR of 60 beats per minute is assigned an exercise
intensity of 60% to 70% of functional capacity:
APMHR = 220 - 30 = 190 beats/min
RHR = 60 beats/min
HRR = 190 - 60 = 130 beats/min
