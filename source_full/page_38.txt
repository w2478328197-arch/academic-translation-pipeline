After completing this chapter, you will be able to
describe both the macrostructure and microstructure of muscle
and bone;
describe the sliding-filament theory of muscular contraction;
describe the specific morphological and physiological
characteristics of different muscle fiber types and predict their
relative involvement in different sport events;
describe the anatomical and physiological characteristics of the
cardiovascular and respiratory systems; and
predict the cardiorespiratory responses to different types of
exercise or sports events.
