Training Goals
The three goals to enhance agility performance through training are
to develop the physical qualities to allow athletes to adopt the
required postures and positions for a range of different agility
movements performed in the sport as well as enhance braking and
propulsion of such tasks (action capacity); to develop movement
efficiency and competency of range of agility movements pertinent
to the athlete’s sport, and to enhance perceptual-cognitive ability in
various situations and tactical scenarios (35).
