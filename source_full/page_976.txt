   8   SEATED ROW (MACHINE)
Starting Position
Sit erect with the feet flat on the platform and press the torso
against the chest pad.
Grasp the handles with a closed grip, either pronated or neutral.
If necessary, adjust the seat height to position the arms
approximately parallel to the floor.
Allow the elbows to extend fully.
All repetitions begin from this position.
