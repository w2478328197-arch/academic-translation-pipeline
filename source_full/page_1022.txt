Squat the bar down until it rests on the supports.
Upward Movement Phase: Two Spotters
Keep the hands cupped and close to—but not touching—the bar
as it ascends.
Slightly extend the knees, hips, and torso, and keep the back
neutral when following the bar.
At the end of the set, move sideways in unison with the athlete
back to the rack.
Simultaneously grasp the bar and assist with balancing the bar
as it is racked.
Release the bar smoothly.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
gluteus maximus, semimembranosus, semitendinosus,
biceps femoris, vastus lateralis, vastus intermedius, vastus
medialis, rectus femoris, biceps brachii
VERBAL CUES
Cradle the bar in your elbows
Keep your elbows in
Control your descent
COMMON ERRORS
Not keeping the elbows close to the body
Leaning too far forward
