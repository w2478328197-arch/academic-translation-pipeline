Starting position: Stand to one side of the plyometric box with one
foot on the ground and one foot on the box. The inside of the foot
on the box should be near the box’s closest edge.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: None or slightly lift the foot off the box.
Upward movement: Jump up using the foot on the box to push off.
Downward movement: Land with the same foot on the box; this foot
should land just before the ground foot. Immediately repeat the
movement.
COMMON ERRORS
Not producing sufficient vertical force from the front foot
Poor coordination or strength deficiencies, which means
that peak height of the center of mass does not occur at
the same time as maximal extension of the lower limbs
and flexion in the shoulders
