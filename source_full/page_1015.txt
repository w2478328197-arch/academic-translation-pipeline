Starting Position: Two Spotters
Stand erect at opposite ends of the bar with the feet shoulder-
width apart and the knees slightly flexed.
Grasp the end of the bar by wrapping the hands around the bar
with the thumbs crossed and palms facing the barbell.
At the athlete’s signal, assist with lifting and balancing the bar
as it is moved off the supports.
Release the bar smoothly.
Hold the hands 2 to 3 inches (5-8 cm) below the ends of the
bar.
