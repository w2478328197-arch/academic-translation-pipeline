Organizing the construction and design of a strength and
conditioning facility requires a well-thought-out plan created by a
committee of experienced professionals. This chapter provides
information on the different phases of planning a new facility, along
with details about facility design, organization, and equipment
maintenance. The chapter also outlines the phases of construction or
remodeling of a facility. The design should account for the needs of
various stakeholders (e.g., athletes, staff, administrators) for the
layout to be most efficient.
