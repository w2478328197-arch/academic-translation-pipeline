  44   ROTATIONAL THROW
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Forward through frontal plane
Equipment: Medicine or plyometric ball weighing 8 to 35 pounds (4-
16 kg); wall
Starting position: Start in a lunge stance. The front leg (with the hip,
knee, and ankle aligned) should face the wall approximately 3 to 6
feet (1-2 m) away. The rear leg should be externally rotated at the
hip so that the foot is flat on the ground and pointing approximately
parallel to the wall. Hold the ball at waist height in front of the body.
