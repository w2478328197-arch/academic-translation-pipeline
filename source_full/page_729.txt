After completing this chapter, you will be able to
identify and explain reasons for performing tests with athletes;
understand testing terminology to communicate clearly with
athletes and colleagues;
evaluate the validity and reliability of a test and its metrics;
select appropriate tests and metrics; and
administer test protocols properly and safely.
