  9   PIKE JUMP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Vertical
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Explosively jump up. Keeping the legs straight
and together, try to lift them to the front and try to touch the toes
with the hands.
Downward 
movement: 
Land in the 
starting 
position, and
immediately repeat the jump.
