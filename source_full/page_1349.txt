Upward movement: Push off with the standing leg to rise vertically
and sideways over the mini-hurdle. Keep the ankle dorsiflexed in
preparation for an active flat-foot contact with the ground on
landing.
Downward movement: Land with the same foot on the opposite side
of the hurdle. Immediately repeat the movement to the opposite
side.
COMMON ERRORS
Not producing sufficient vertical force from the front foot
to push out of the squat position and clear the hurdle
with full extension in the hip and knee
Absorbing too much of the landing impact into the hip
and knee of the front leg, increasing ground contact time
