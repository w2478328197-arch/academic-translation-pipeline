Student and Professional Resources in
HKPropel
If you purchased a new print book, follow the directions included on
the card at the front of your book to access the online content
included with this new edition. If you purchased an e-book from
HumanKinetics.com, follow the access instructions that were e-
mailed to you following your purchase. If you purchased a used
book, you may purchase a new access code by visiting
US.HumanKinetics.com and searching for “Essentials of Strength
Training and Conditioning HKPropel Access.”
The online content includes a collection of video clips of 25
resistance training exercises for use in understanding and
performing correct exercise technique. Plus, lab activities are
provided to give students hands-on practice with testing and
evaluation. The 20 forms make completing and submitting lab
assignments easy.
