  15   SIDE BEND WITH BENT ARM
1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Flex the right elbow and raise the elbow above the head.
3. Reach the right hand down toward the left shoulder.
4. Grasp the right elbow with the left hand.
5. Pull the elbow behind the head.
6. Keeping the elbow flexed, lean from the waist to the left side.
Do not flex the knees.
7. Repeat on the other side.
