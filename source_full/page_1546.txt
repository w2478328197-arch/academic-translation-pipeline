Comments
Frequency. To help combat overtraining or overuse, the aerobic training days should
be spread out evenly during the week to allow recovery between sessions.
Duration. Since the athlete’s race distance is a marathon, the duration or running
distance of the extensive endurance training sessions should progressively approach
80% to 85% that of the marathon, at least for one of the two aerobic (extensive
endurance) sessions.
Intensity. To complete the extensive endurance sessions, the athlete should run at a
low intensity or a slow training pace; high respiratory stress should be avoided. The
aerobic runs should be at zone 1 on the three-zone intensity scale (figure 21.1 on
page 701) or zone 2 on the five-zone intensity scale (table 21.1 on page 697).
