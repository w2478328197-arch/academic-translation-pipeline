Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance with feet
hip-width apart.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Explosively jump up. Pull the knees to the chest,
quickly grasp the knees with both hands, and release before landing.
Pull the ankles toward the knees in flight to prepare for an active
flat-foot landing.
Downward 
movement: 
Land in the 
starting 
position, and
immediately repeat the jump.
COMMON ERRORS
Pulling the knees up rather than pushing into the ground
maximally then tucking knees
Failure to perform a countermovement on subsequent
repetitions; this decreases ground contact time but
reduces vertical force generation
