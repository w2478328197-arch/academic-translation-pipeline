Simultaneously grasp the bar and assist with balancing the bar
as it is racked.
Release the bar smoothly.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
gluteus maximus, semimembranosus, semitendinosus,
biceps femoris, vastus lateralis, vastus intermedius, vastus
medialis, rectus femoris
VERBAL CUES
Chest up, head neutral
Spread the floor with your feet
Drive through your heels
COMMON ERRORS
Allowing the heels to lift off the floor, the torso to flex
further forward, or the upper back to round during the
upward phase of the movement
Allowing the knees to move in (via hip adduction) or out
(via hip abduction) during the movement
