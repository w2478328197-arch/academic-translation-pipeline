  30   REAR FOOT–ELEVATED
MULTIDIRECTIONAL HOP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Vertical and lateral
Equipment: Plyometric box, 6 to 18 inches (15-46 cm) high; mini-
hurdle, 6 to 24 inches (15-61 cm) high
Starting position: Start in a deep split squat position, with the rear
foot elevated on a plyometric box (or equivalent). The foot on the
ground should rest parallel to the mini-hurdle.
Preparatory movement: None
