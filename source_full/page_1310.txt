  6   SPLIT SQUAT JUMP
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Vertical
Starting position: Get into a lunge position with one leg forward (hip
and knee joints flexed approximately 90 degrees) and the other
behind the midline of the body with the trunk vertical.
Arm action: Double arm or none
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement.
Upward movement: Explosively jump up, using the arms to assist as
needed. Maximum height and power should be emphasized.
Downward movement: Dorsiflex the ankles in preparation for an
active flat-foot landing. (Note: The second photo does not yet show
the feet positioned for landing.) When landing, maintain the lunge
position (with the same leg forward) and immediately repeat the
jump. After completing a set, rest and switch front legs.
