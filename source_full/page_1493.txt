  5   DRIBBLES
This drill, which assists with many aspects of the technical running
model involving a scaled running practice, emphasizes the more
circular path of the foot during maximum-velocity sprinting.
Starting Position
Stand with the feet hip-width apart and the head and trunk
upright.
Movement Phase
Lift one leg so the foot is above the opposing ankle with toes
pointed up, then sweep the foot down and back to make foot
contact. As the center of mass moves ahead of the foot, the
foot should drive up and over the ankle. Repeat this circular
foot motion with each limb for the designated distance. The
