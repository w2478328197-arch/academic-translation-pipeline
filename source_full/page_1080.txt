Note: The mid-thigh pull can be performed with loads greater than
the loads that are used when performing the power clean or power
snatch. In fact, it is not uncommon for athletes to be able to perform
the mid-thigh pulls with as much as 140% of the 1RM.
MAJOR MUSCLES INVOLVED
gluteus maximus, semimembranosus, semitendinosus,
biceps femoris, vastus lateralis, vastus intermedius, vastus
medialis, rectus femoris, soleus, gastrocnemius, deltoids,
trapezius
VERBAL CUES
Drive the feet through the floor
Stay on your heels as long as possible
Shrug vertically with long arms
COMMON ERRORS
Not maintaining a vertical trunk position resulting in the
hips being too high and the shoulder being placed in
front of the bar. (Note: often a result of initiating the lift
from the wrong position.)
Performing the shrug motion before completing the
triple extension of the lower body
Flexing the elbows before completing the triple
extension of the lower body
