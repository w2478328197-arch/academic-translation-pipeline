  28   ALTERNATING-LEG PUSH-OFF
Intensity level: Low
Direction: Vertical
Equipment: Plyometric box, 6 to 18 inches (15-46 cm) high
Starting position: Stand facing the plyometric box with one foot on
the ground and one foot on the box. The heel of the foot on the box
should be near the box’s closest edge.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Lift the front foot slightly so that it can be
driven down into the box.
Upward movement: Drive the front foot down into the box to cause
an upward jumping action.
Downward movement: Land with the opposite foot on the box; this
foot should land just before the ground foot. Immediately repeat the
movement, reversing the feet each repetition.
