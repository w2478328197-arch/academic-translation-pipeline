  22   WALL STRETCH
1. Stand facing the wall with feet shoulder-width apart and toes
approximately 2 feet (0.6 m) from the wall.
2. Lean forward, placing the hands on the wall.
3. Step back approximately 2 feet (0.6 m) with the stretch leg
while flexing the opposite knee.
4. Extend the knee of the stretch leg and lower the heel to the
floor to apply the stretch.
5. Repeat on the other side.
