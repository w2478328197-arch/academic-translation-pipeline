  16   LINEAR HOP
Intensity level: Medium
Direction: Horizontal and vertical
Starting position: Get into a comfortable, upright stance on one foot.
The non-jumping leg is held in a stationary position with the hip and
knee flexed during the exercise.
Arm action: Double arm
Preparatory movement: Begin with a countermovement on the
hopping leg. Bring the other leg behind the body.
Upward movement: Explosively hop forward, driving from the hip,
knee, and ankle, using both arms and the opposite knee to assist
the movement.

active flat-foot landing. (Note: The second photo does not yet show
