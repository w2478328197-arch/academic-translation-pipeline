bodies (e.g., NCAA, United States Olympic Committee, Major League
Baseball, National Basketball Association, National Football League,
National Hockey League). It is the responsibility of the strength and
conditioning professional to stay up to date and seek out the related
rules and regulations, as well as the scientific evidence that supports
or refutes the efficacy of nutritional supplements. It is recommended
that strength and conditioning professionals consult with a sports
nutritionist or dietitian on matters regarding nutritional supplements.
