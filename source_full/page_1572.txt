  6   WALKING (GAIT)
Body Position
Hold the head upright with the eyes looking straight ahead.
Relax the shoulders, and do not allow them to round.
Position the upper body directly over the hips while keeping the
ear, shoulder, and hip aligned.
Foot Strike
The heel should strike the ground first, followed by a gentle
rolling heel-to-ball action, allowing the weight to be spread over
the foot.
The weight should transfer from the outer side of the heel and
continue to shift forward and slightly inward toward the middle
of the ball of the foot at push-off.
