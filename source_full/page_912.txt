Anterior of Thigh and Hip Flexor
  17   SIDE QUADRICEPS STRETCH
1. Lie on the right side with both legs straight.
2. Place the right forearm flat on the floor and the upper arm
perpendicular to the floor.
3. Place the left forearm at a 45-degree angle to the torso.
4. Flex the left leg (knee), with the heel of the left foot moving
toward the buttocks.
5. Grasp the front of the ankle with the left hand and pull toward
the buttocks.
6. Repeat on the other side.
Note: The stretch occurs as a result of knee flexion and hip
extension.
MUSCLES AFFECTED
quadriceps, iliopsoas
